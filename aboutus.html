About Us

From the vibrant streets of Gadigal Land, Social Strangers are blazing their own trail in the Alt-Rock scene.
Fronted by a powerhouse female vocalist and fuelled by electrifying guitar riffs and heavy-hitting bass their
music is a fusion of raw energy and immersive storytelling.

Drawing inspiration from grunge, punk, post-grunge, emo rock, and nu Metal, Social Strangers deliver a sound
that is both familiar and refreshingly unique.

Their live performances are nothing short of a spectacle, igniting stages with an infectious energy that
lingers after the final note is struck.
